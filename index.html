About FindIt

Lost items on campus go unrecovered because there is no central system. FindIt changes that.

The Problem

Notice boards and group chats are scattered. Items go unclaimed for days with no way to connect finders and owners.

The Solution

A single platform to report, match, verify, and return lost items — accessible to all students and staff.

The Goal

Higher recovery rates, less manual effort, and a transparent, trustworthy process for the entire campus.

Features

Everything needed to get a lost item back to its owner.

Lost Item Reporting

Submit a report with name, location, date, and photos. Get a unique tracking ID.

Found Item Reporting

Log a found item in seconds. It's matched against active lost reports automatically.

Smart Matching

Compares item type, location, and date to find and rank potential matches.

Claim Verification

Ownership is confirmed via security questions before any item is released.

Notifications

Get alerted when a match is found or when your claim status is updated.

QR Recovery

Attach a QR sticker to valuables. Scanning it notifies you instantly — no app needed.

FAQ

How do I report a lost item?

Click Report Lost Item, fill in the form, and submit. You'll receive a tracking ID to follow up.

What happens after I submit a report?

FindIt scans existing found-item reports and alerts you if a potential match is found, ranked by confidence.

How does matching work?

The system compares item category, description, location, and date to score potential matches automatically.

Who can use FindIt?

Any student, faculty member, or staff at the college. Each can report, claim, or hand over found items.

How is ownership verified?

Claimants answer questions about the item that only the real owner would know, before the handover is approved.

What is QR Code Recovery?

Register a valuable and print a QR sticker. Any finder can scan it to notify you directly — no account needed.
